About us

We are Menno and Rachel van Rookhuijzen, with out boys Ezra, Corban and Oliver. We're based in Perth Australia and work with Youth With A Mission (YWAM) Perth. We've been here in Perth for the last 8 or so years and have been working in different areas of ministry with YWAM Perth. For the majority of the time that has been midwifery and media/IT. We're planning on being here at least 2 more years and we'll see how God leads us after that. Just this last week our family has expanded, we are now a family of 5. So the next few weeks we'll be getting used to that. To find us on social media see links below.
